### Controls::
<br> `space:activate hover`
<br> `left:left`
<br> `right:right`
<br> `up:jump`
<br> `down:duck`
<br> `l:toggle flash`
<br> `b:toggle greyscale`
<br> `cheatcode::can go through topleft and topright of pillar`
